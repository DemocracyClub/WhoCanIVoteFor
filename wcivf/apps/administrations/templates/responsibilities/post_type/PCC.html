{% load markdown_filter %} {# fmt:off #} {% filter markdown %} Police and crime commissioners are elected in areas of England and Wales to make sure that local police meet the needs of the community. They are responsible for: * how your area is policed * the police budget * the amount of Council Tax charged for the police * the information you get about what the local police are doing * appointing and dismissing the chief constable (the most senior police officer for the area) {% endfilter %}
