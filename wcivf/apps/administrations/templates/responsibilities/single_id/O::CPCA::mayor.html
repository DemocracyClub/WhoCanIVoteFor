{% load markdown_filter %} {# fmt:off #} {% filter markdown %} The role of the Mayor of Cambridgeshire and Peterborough is to deliver economic prosperity across the region, to make Cambridgeshire and Peterborough a leading place in the world to live, learn, work and do business. The Mayor oversees a £20 million annual budget devolved from government, and also has devolved powers to spend up to £800 million on local housing, infrastructure and jobs.
